📌 Smart Alumni Management System

📖 Overview

The Smart Alumni Management System is a full-stack platform designed to strengthen the relationship between alumni and their alma mater. It provides a centralized ecosystem where alumni can connect, collaborate, contribute, and grow professionally.

This platform solves common challenges faced by alumni associations such as low engagement, lack of networking opportunities, and inefficient donation systems.

⸻

🚀 Features

🔐 Alumni Registration
	•	Easy sign-up and login system
	•	Profile creation & updates
	•	Secure authentication

💰 Donation Portal
	•	Seamless and secure donation system
	•	Support for institutional initiatives
	•	Transparent contribution tracking

🌐 Networking Hub
	•	Connect alumni based on:
	•	Profession
	•	Interests
	•	Location
	•	Mentorship & collaboration opportunities

💼 Job Portal
	•	Alumni can:
	•	Post job openings
	•	Apply for jobs
	•	Internal hiring within alumni network

📚 Alumni Directory
	•	Advanced search filters:
	•	Graduation year
	•	Department
	•	Industry
	•	Location

🏆 Success Stories
	•	Showcase alumni achievements
	•	Inspire students and community

🎉 Events & Reunions
	•	Event announcements
	•	Registration system
	•	Alumni meetups & workshops

📊 Feedback & Surveys
	•	Collect alumni feedback
	•	Improve platform experience
	•	Conduct surveys
